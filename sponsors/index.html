Amsterdam JSNation is a full day, two-track conference covering the latest and greatest news and insights from the global Javascript network. For the second year in a row already we will be bringing JS professionals under the single flag here in Amsterdam.

Conference 1st edition back in summer 2017 involved 250+ attendees from 15 countries, 20+ speakers, 16 sponsors and was held at one of the most unique Amsterdam locations - ARTIS Natura Magistra. This year we are coming back with an ambitious plan to step up our game on all levels and hope you will join us among other industry leaders on this ambitious journey.

Whenever you are looking to put your company name in the spotlight, rally up the team knowledge or hire the best talent - we have a matching proposal.
